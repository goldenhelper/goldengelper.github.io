About Me

I'm a mathematician with a passion for making complex concepts intuitive through dynamic visualizations. With a background in applied mathematics and computer science, I bridge the gap between abstract mathematical ideas and their practical applications.

My research focuses on dynamical systems, differential geometry, and their applications in machine learning. I believe that mathematical literacy is crucial in our data-driven world, and I'm dedicated to creating educational content that makes these concepts accessible.
